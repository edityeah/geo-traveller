# Vistara now offers direct flights from Mumbai to Mauritius

**Date:** 2023-04-02  
**Slug:** vistara-direct-flights-from-mumbai-to-mauritius  
**Original:** https://geo-traveller.com/vistara-direct-flights-from-mumbai-to-mauritius/

**Blocks:** 15

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-810c-8634-ff0ba8f51285 type:paragraph -->
Vistara, an Indian airline, has recently launched direct flights between Mumbai and Mauritius, making Mauritius its 15th international destination and first in Africa. The airline is operating five flights per week and has deployed its A321LR aircraft with a three-class configuration, which includes Business, Premium Economy, and Economy classes.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8115-902e-cb473d106bb7 type:paragraph -->
Vinod Kannan, the CEO of Vistara, has expressed his excitement about the airline's entry into Africa and said that the strong trade ties and long-standing relations between India and Mauritius, along with the potential for tourism in the region, make it a perfect fit for Vistara's growing network. He also mentioned that Vistara is the only carrier offering Premium Economy on this route, which will provide customers with a range of options to choose from.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81bc-bc6a-d828aed32eb5 type:paragraph -->
Vistara is known for its award-winning product and services, and it is expected that customers will appreciate the airline's offerings in this new sector. With this launch, Vistara has further expanded its international footprint and has shown its commitment to providing world-class services to its customers.
