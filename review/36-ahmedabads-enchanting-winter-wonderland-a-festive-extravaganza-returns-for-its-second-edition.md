<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81da-a027-e35227230934 type:heading_1 -->
# Ahmedabad's Winter Wonderland: A Festive Spectacle

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8182-afee-ddb48962d578 type:paragraph -->
Toycra's Winter Wonderland is a Christmas celebration that brings together local and national brands to create a magical experience for children and families alike. Scheduled for December 16 and 17, 2023, at the Merriment Party Plot, SBR, the event is a testament to Ahmedabad's vibrant culture and spirit. With over 2,000 attendees last year, this year's edition is poised to be an even grander affair.

## Attractions for the Young and Young at Heart

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81f7-b09f-c5d272734caa type:paragraph -->
The fair boasts an array of attractions for children, with brands like Lego, Toycra, and Paw Patrol creating a wonderland of experiences. The event offers all-inclusive fun activities that promise to ignite imagination and captivate hearts. Parents can relax and soak in the festive atmosphere while their children engage in creative and playful activities.
