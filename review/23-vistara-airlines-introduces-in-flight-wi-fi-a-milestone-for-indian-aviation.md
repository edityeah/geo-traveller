In-flight Wi-Fi has become an essential feature for modern travelers, allowing them to stay connected while soaring at 35,000 feet. Vistara Airlines, a renowned carrier under Tata Sons and SIA, has set a remarkable precedent by becoming the first Indian airline to offer complimentary in-flight Wi-Fi services to its Club Vistara members. In this article, we'll explore this exciting development and its significance for passengers.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81a6-96dc-dce0917e2582 type:heading_1 -->
# Vistara's Complimentary In-Flight Wi-Fi

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8198-b73f-f3ea6647a595 type:embed -->
[embed: https://twitter.com/airvistara/status/1720056016354893996](https://twitter.com/airvistara/status/1720056016354893996)

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81aa-bba2-c670b7348333 type:paragraph -->
Vistara Airlines is proud to announce that its Club Vistara members can now enjoy the convenience of in-flight messaging services. Passengers can use popular platforms like WhatsApp, Messenger, and iMessage, even while cruising at high altitudes. This makes Vistara the exclusive Indian airline to provide this valuable service on board. It's worth noting that passengers will be able to send text messages only and won't have the capability to exchange images, videos, or audio files.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8183-ae90-e93d6ac7e1a6 type:paragraph -->
For Vistara CV Platinum and Business Class customers, an added perk is the provision of free 50MB data for onboard internet browsing. To access both benefits, members are required to activate the 50MB surf plan before enabling the unlimited texting service.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8193-9826-d7e86bb72cf5 type:paragraph -->
This fantastic amenity is available when flying internationally on Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner™ and Airbus A321 aircraft. Passengers must ensure that their CV ID is linked to their booking to enjoy uninterrupted messaging throughout the flight.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-816a-b3b5-da5c58bf9f45 type:paragraph -->
To qualify for the Complimentary Wi-Fi, members must have completed their enrollment and verified their contact details, including email and mobile number. Additionally, it's essential that the CV ID matches the name on the ticket for the messaging service to be accessible. Please note that this offer is subject to change and holds no monetary value.

# The Significance of In-Flight Messaging Services

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81e2-9407-e4b6caca7b1d type:embed -->
[embed: https://twitter.com/airvistara/status/1713065899106922581](https://twitter.com/airvistara/status/1713065899106922581)

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-813c-873e-d2de175dc8ed type:paragraph -->
In-flight messaging services have gained popularity due to their ability to keep passengers connected with loved ones, colleagues, and friends, even when they are thousands of feet above the ground. For business travelers, in-flight messaging is indispensable for staying productive, allowing them to work, respond to emails, and collaborate during the flight. Furthermore, messaging serves as a form of entertainment, enhancing the overall flying experience.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-811e-9fa1-c54a576a1de8 type:paragraph -->
From an airline's perspective, offering in-flight messaging services can be a lucrative endeavor. Passengers are often willing to pay for in-flight Wi-Fi access, and messaging services can be bundled with internet packages or offered as a standalone service. Airlines that provide reliable in-flight messaging gain a competitive edge, as passengers choose carriers based on the availability and quality of in-flight connectivity options.
