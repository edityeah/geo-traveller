# KGAF 2024: Mumbai's Artistic Heart Beats Again at the Kala Ghoda Arts Festival

The Kala Ghoda Arts Festival (KGAF), Asia’s largest multi-disciplinary street arts festival, is all set to bedazzle Mumbai once again. Mark your calendars for January 20th to January 28th, 2024, as KGAF returns for its 24th edition, themed ‘Udaan’. This iconic festival, a brainchild of the Kala Ghoda Association, is not just an event; it's a cultural odyssey set in the bustling heart of Mumbai, forming an integral part of the Mumbai Festival 2024.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8152-a42a-d9d678e3fe18 type:heading_1 -->
# A Kaleidoscope of Artistic Endeavors

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8114-9956-d607d1547df1 type:paragraph -->
Expect to be swept off your feet with over 300 programmes spanning 14 diverse verticals. KGAF 2024 will transform over 25 venues in Mumbai’s Kala Ghoda precinct into an artistic paradise. From visual arts to dance, music, theatre, literature, and more, the festival promises a rich tapestry of cultural experiences.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-811e-be65-dcee1469f201 type:heading_1 -->
# Star-Studded Line-up and Unforgettable Experiences

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-818f-8c3c-c988fc085a4b type:paragraph -->
This year's festival boasts an impressive line-up of artists like Javed Akhtar, Farhan Akhtar, and Herbie Hancock, along with emerging talents. Whether you're a connoisseur of the arts or a casual enthusiast, there's something for everyone at KGAF 2024.
