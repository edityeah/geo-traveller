However, it's not just their cricketing prowess that's capturing attention; it's their acts of kindness, too. A heartwarming instance involves Rahmanullah Gurbaz, a member of the team, who was seen extending a helping hand to the less fortunate on the streets of Ahmedabad.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-810d-8873-f7111b173152 type:heading_1 -->
# Rahmanullah Gurbaz's Act of Kindness Goes Viral

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81c3-8e4b-f3616b78fe2c type:paragraph -->
A video shared on X (previously known as Twitter) by user Mufaddal Vohra (@mufaddal_vohra) shows Gurbaz quietly aiding people sleeping on the footpaths. The wicketkeeper-batter was captured distributing money to the homeless around 3 AM near Doordarshan Cross Road. Gurbaz, without disturbing their sleep, discreetly placed money beside them and left the scene.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8104-b202-e7f2bf9e7b6f type:paragraph -->
This act, especially significant during the festive time of Diwali, showcased Gurbaz's compassion and humility. His gesture has not only brightened the lives of those he helped but has also left an indelible mark on the hearts of many.
