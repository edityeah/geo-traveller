The Bangalore Literature Festival (BLF), a gem in the cultural and literary landscape of Bangalore, is back with its 12th edition.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-810c-a35b-cd125a559a11 type:heading_1 -->
# What's new in BLF 2023?

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8133-9160-ed0e8b878aa4 type:paragraph -->
The upcoming edition of BLF promises an enriching literary experience, with a lineup of over 250 esteemed writers. Notable figures like Perumal Murugan, Chandrashekhara Kambara, Abraham Verghese, and Ramachandra Guha will grace the event, bringing together a mix of fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and regional literature.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8190-abb9-c964f274ba69 type:heading_1 -->
# Highlights of the Bangalore Literature Festival

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-816a-947a-cb1a5f9ebb5d type:paragraph -->
BLF 2023 is set to dazzle with its variety of sessions. Key attractions include a keynote by Dr. Abraham Verghese, a thought-provoking discussion on Jainism with Devdutt Pattanaik, and a unique meet-and-greet with Sudha Murty and her canine character Gopi. The festival will also feature an enlightening session on language by Shashi Tharoor and his sister, Smita Tharoor, and a captivating dialogue with Prahlad Kakar.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8198-ad98-c2ffd30ca6c1 type:heading_1 -->
# For the Young and Young at Heart

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-816a-bfa1-d0e210fdf0b2 type:paragraph -->
The festival isn't just for adults; a kid-only carousel will captivate young minds. Additionally, the LitMart and ScreenLit segments offer a platform for aspiring writers to pitch their novel and screenplay ideas. The evenings will come alive with musical performances by Kavish Seth and Avrina Prabala-Joslin.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81f8-9bf6-f46398fb9a3b type:heading_1 -->
# The Significance of Literary Festivals

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81b0-8350-ca668ae9b599 type:paragraph -->
BLF, while celebrating global literature, remains deeply rooted in local culture. These festivals are not just about literature; they are incubators of cultural and intellectual growth. They encourage a reading culture, promote cultural tourism, and serve as venues for discussing societal issues.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-810a-bbe1-e42b6d8799cd type:paragraph -->
Don't miss the chance to be part of the Bangalore Literature Festival's 12th edition, a celebration of literature, culture, and intellectual discourse.
