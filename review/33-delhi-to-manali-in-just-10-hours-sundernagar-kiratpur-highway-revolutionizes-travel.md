Journeying from Delhi's urban hustle to the serene beauty of Manali has become swifter and more enjoyable. The inauguration of the Sundernagar-Kiratpur four-lane highway has significantly reduced the travel time, making it a 10-hour smooth drive.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8153-9327-c26232c22b7c type:heading_1 -->
# Sundernagar-Kiratpur Four-Lane: A Game-Changer for Commuters

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8104-a9d7-d5a6bad6ab9f type:paragraph -->
With the National Highway Authority of India (NHAI) opening the Sundernagar-Harabagh to Kiratpur section, the drive between Delhi and Manali has been transformed. This new route trims down the bus travel duration by two hours, a substantial improvement for travelers.

# New Routes and Convenience for Travelers

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8172-bd1a-cb70d96cd9ec type:paragraph -->
The Transport Corporation has rerouted several buses to leverage this new highway. Luxury buses now travel via Bilaspur, Goni, and Swarghat, bypassing the previous congested routes. Additionally, these buses utilize the Ner Chowk bypass, effectively evading traffic snarls.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8107-b5ed-e006eab1cd15 type:paragraph -->
Passengers heading to Ner Chowk can opt for alternative routes through Dadaur village in Mandi district. The luxury buses from Manali to Delhi, Chandigarh, and Haridwar have also adapted their schedules to incorporate this faster, more efficient highway.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81f9-a28e-ff3148b93861 type:heading_1 -->
# Manali in Winter: A Mesmerizing Destination

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81ee-8442-d9a15e5700b6 type:paragraph -->
Beyond the benefits of the new highway, Manali during winter presents an enchanting world. The town, nestled by the Beas River and surrounded by snowy peaks, offers an array of winter sports in Solang Valley, from skiing to snowmobiling. The natural hot springs like Vashisht and Manikaran add to the allure, providing a unique mountain retreat experience.
