Delhi, a city celebrated for its vibrant street food scene, is all set to host the grandest gastronomic and musical gala – the Delhi Is Cooking Festival, sponsored by Coca-Cola. This event promises to be a delightful treat for all food and music lovers.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8124-8e01-ca47293dc5e7 type:heading_1 -->
# Coca-Cola's Delhi Is Cooking: A Celebration of Flavors and Tunes

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81e0-85d4-d9d72174ca20 type:paragraph -->
Mark your calendars for October 27 and 28 and head to the NSIC Grounds in Okhla to dive into this lavish festivity. The festival, known for its blend of humor and excitement, will be hosted by the charismatic comedian Abish Mathew.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8115-bca4-fdead0153b67 type:paragraph -->
Over the two days, attendees will have the chance to indulge in an array of delicious dishes from over 50 acclaimed food stalls. And what’s a meal without a refreshing bottle of Coca-Cola to complement it?

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8185-8700-d4593243f61b type:paragraph -->
But it's not just about the food. The festival also offers a variety of engaging games and activities, ensuring endless fun for everyone.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81e2-a3af-ce1fea3b964e type:heading_1 -->
# A Musical Rendezvous at Delhi Is Cooking

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81c3-9ba9-f3586788faa8 type:image -->
![image](https://geo-traveller.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/image-14-1024x559.png)

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8144-848a-c63a037f79fd type:paragraph -->
Prepare to be enthralled by over 10 celebrated Indian musicians. The line-up includes Naalayak, Raman Negi, Euphoria, Raftaar, Nucleya on Saturday, and Kamakshi Khanna, Srushti Tawade, Rashmeet Kaur, Seedhe Maut, and Prateek Kuhad on Sunday.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-81be-8b00-d8ce74e9a89b type:paragraph -->
Adding to the excitement, more than 20 popular influencers like Shivesh Bhatia, Gurpreet Singh Tikku, and Anunay Sood will be gracing the event, making it an unmissable weekend of fun, flavors, and the irreplaceable charm of Coca-Cola.
