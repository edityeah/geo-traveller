The Indian Railways is loaded with lots of spectacular things for those who want to explore India’s rich cultural heritage and glorified historical sites.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-819e-8284-c5598a7bdda1 type:paragraph -->
The package includes visits to Ayodhya, Prayagraj, Varanasi, and Vaishno Devi.

<!-- block:37a7bc30-b890-8179-9d5c-c7bf8a4fe08b type:paragraph -->
The name of the package is Uttar Darshan by Bharat Gaurav Special Train, which will take you on a spiritual journey, visiting some of the most famous temples of India. The tour is set to begin from 27th May 2023 and end on 6th June 2023. You can board the Bharat Gaurav Tourist Train from seven different stations, including Dibrugarh, Mariani, Lumding, Guwahati, New Cooch Behar, New Jalpaiguri, and Katihar. The cost of the tour package is INR 20,850 per person for the economy category, and INR 31,135 for the standard category.
